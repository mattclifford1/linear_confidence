4. Relevant Information:
   The dataset was collected at 'Hospital Universitario de Caracas' in Caracas, Venezuela. 
   The dataset comprises demographic information, habits, and historic medical records 
   of 858 patients. Several patients decided not to answer some of the questions because 
   of privacy concerns (missing values).
